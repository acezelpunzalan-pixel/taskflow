Section 2
Field Visit → Invoice Process Flow
End-to-end flow from a field visit being logged to the invoice being paid.
🔧
Field Visit Occurs
A third-party vendor performs work at a site. The visit is logged in the Field Work page with vendor name, site, description, and date.
📍 Field Work page
🧾
Invoice Received from Vendor
The third party sends an invoice. The invoice number, amount, and due date are logged in the field visit record. The invoice is also imported (or manually entered) into the Invoices page for tracking.
🧾 Invoices page · Status: Submitted
👁
Review & Verification
The invoice is reviewed against the visit record — amount, site, scope of work. PO number is cross-checked if applicable.
✓ Matches visit record Proceed to approval
✕ Discrepancy found Mark as Disputed — add comment with reason
✓
Approval
Invoice is approved via the Invoices detail panel. Comment logged to the approval trail with date. Invoice status changes to Approved.
✓ Status: Approved
💸
Payment Processed
Payment is made externally. Invoice is marked Paid in the system. The aging bar clears and the amount moves from Unpaid Balance to Total Paid.
$ Status: Paid
📊
Reporting & Export
At any time, all records can be exported to CSV from both the Field Work and Invoices pages for accounting, reporting, or auditing purposes.
⬇ Export CSV
🔁
Cycle Repeats
The vendor returns for the next visit. The site remains managed in Admin, and the vendor name autocompletes from historical records.
